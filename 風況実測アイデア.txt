2. Callback Execution Phase (Inside the returned `wind_profile(z)` closure):

   - **Clipping/Extrapolation:** If `z` is below the minimum or above the maximum GPV height, use the exact vector values of the nearest boundary (do not throw an error).
   - **Recombination:** Convert the interpolated U/V vectors back to speed (using `math.hypot`) and direction (using `math.atan2`, converted back to degrees).

   - **Offset (Direction):** - `\theta_calc(z) = (obs_dir + (\theta_gpv(z) - \theta_gpv(10))) % 360`
     - Ensure the final angle is strictly wrapped between 0.0 and 360.0.
